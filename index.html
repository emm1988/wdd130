La Paz - Bolivia

La Paz, is a fascinating city high in the Andes mountains at around 3,650 meters above sea level. The city's rich blend of modern life and indigenous traditions. The cable car system provides spectacular views of the city and surrounding mountains. La Paz has several notable tourist sites that offer unique experience, some of those are: Valle de la Luna (Valley of the Moon) a surreal landscape that looks like the surface of the moon, with unusual rock formations created by erosion, and Lake Titicaca, located a few hours from La Paz, it's the highest navigable lake in the world. The pictures scenery and the mystical Isla del Sol make it a must-visit.
